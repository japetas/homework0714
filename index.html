With cap space teams like the Brooklyn Nets, Atlanta Hawks and Chicago Bulls making moves, it appears as though the Oklahoma City Thunder will be unable to trade away the contract of Carmelo Anthony. "At some point here, it is expected they'll do a waive-and-stretch on Carmelo Anthony," said Adrian Wojnarowski. The Thunder will receive significant luxury tax savings by using the stretch provision on Anthony. Anthony is widely expected to sign with the Houston Rockets when he becomes a free agent.
